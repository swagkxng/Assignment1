My Personality

According to my personality test, I am Logistician.

I pride myself in being well-organized and dependable; hence, my full strength in academic life is my professionalism to work and see that everything that is assigned will be done in the best way. I am selective about details, and it is something positive for me because I am never late, always remember the deadlines, keep my work quality high, and error-free. This approach makes me an efficient student and allows me to meet the challenges of doing complex work while bearing in mind the pressure that comes with that.

Sensing: This makes me functional because it relays calmness during the most tension-filled academic times. However, this sometimes limits my ability to understand and lend myself emotionally to a group project as I might with the rest of the group. Nevertheless, commitment and hard work make me stand out as a responsible team member. While I sometimes only give lip service to the no use of keywords, I further support this by being more responsible and orderly, especially when it comes to handling group works.

In sum, I think that this personality test is valid, accurately depicting me in a true sense. It particularly reflects how organizations I need in school. I do concur with its evaluation of my difficulties, which include when it comes to exhibiting feelings, though on noting my concerns in school life, I would like to think that my tenacity and concentration outweigh every prospect of drawback.

My Hero
