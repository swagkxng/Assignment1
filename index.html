About Me

My name is Jordan Glaves, and I’m currently a student at the University of Technology (Utech). I’m really passionate about gaming and love spending time with my friends. We often hang out, share our latest game experiences, or just unwind together. I’m also deeply interested in computer science and always looking for opportunities to expand my knowledge in the field. There’s something fascinating about understanding how technology works and exploring its endless possibilities.

In my downtime, I love getting lost in a good book, especially if it’s adventure, fantasy, or sci-fi. There’s nothing like immersing myself in other worlds and letting my imagination run wild. Music is another big part of my life; whether I’m studying, relaxing, or just enjoying some quiet time, there’s always a soundtrack playing in the background. It’s amazing how it can set the mood and enhance any experience.
